Support Boundaries

Rooted 21 provides preparation, organization, parenting education, resource navigation, and AI-assisted support. It does not provide legal advice, medical advice, therapy, emergency services, court filing services, or guaranteed outcomes. If there is immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a trusted crisis resource.
